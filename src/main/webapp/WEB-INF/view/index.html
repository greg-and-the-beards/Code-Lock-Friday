Welcome to TAP THIS!


Your one stop shop for all things beer... as long as all you're shopping for is searching for beers and then reviewing them!


  • To register, please use the blue "Register" button on the top left.
  • To Search for a beer or brewery enter the name of either into the "Search" bar, also on the top left.
  • To leave a review for a beer after you've registered (or logged in), click on the name of your chosen beer after you've searched for it and fill in the review form below the beer description.
  • After you're logged in, use the three buttons on the top right to either view the reviews you have submitted, edit your profile information, or log out.
